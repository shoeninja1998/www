Animated Works

The following are animated works done by talented individuals and teams. I'll be using these works to showcase different principles, explaining how they improve the medium.

Diamond Jack

Rachel Kim

A digitally-made animation drawn solely by Rachel Kim, this video hit over 35 million view on Youtube.
This work is oozing with appeal. The characters are charming, with big eyes & defining colors.

The playful attitude from the protagonist as the focal point of the appeal. His smiling makes his robbery feel like a fun game, inviting the police to come along.

Astartes

Syama Pedersen

This is a fanmade animated series based on the world of Warhammer 40k. Acclaimed for its masterful use of 3d animation, Pedersen's work has become part of the official Warhammer 40k website. His use of Staging engages the audience in a fun, unique way.

Pedersen frames his scenes with concision in mind. Most of the time, the only visible object in a shot is the focal point. This efficiently communicates to the audience what is important.

A Brief Disagreement

Steve Cutts

This satirical animation can be only described as chaos. What starts as a "brief" disagreement rapidly involves into warfare across the eras. The use of solid drawing is very evident in this work; the audience is constantly shown different perspectives of one figure, almost as if the video was taken from a single recording of a flying camera.
